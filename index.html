What kind of learner are you?

We experience the world through all our senses. However, you probably tend to favor one of them, although we resort to the others every now and then. Check which kind ofl learner you are predominantly.

Highest score in group 1: You are probably a visual learner. Chances are that you prefer taking in information through your eyes. You probably like to see things written down, read books, looks at pictures, illustrations, and diagrams. Your notes are organized and neat so that you can check them in the future.

Highest score in group 2: You are probably an auditory learner. You probably like to get information through your ears. You appreciate hearing things and listening to audio recordings. Chances are that you also like to repeat things in your head. During classes, you'd probably rather listen to the teacher than take notes.

Highest score in group 3: You are probably a kinesthetic learner. You probably like to get information through your hands, body or emotions. You probably like to touch things, and be in movement. You also like to take notes, not necessarily to look at them again, but because the movement of your hand across paper helps you keep the information you are studying.
